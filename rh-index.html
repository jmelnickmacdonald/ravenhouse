Our story

We found ourselves owners of a nearly 200-year-old home. It felt less like moving in and more like stepping into a living history.

In 2023, we moved to the Annapolis Valley, just outside Greenwood. Ravens circled the driveway on moving day, as if waiting.

We traded commutes for river walks, decades-old spruce, and the way the valley changes with the seasons. The farm has taught us more than we expected.

Here we are raising our children to notice, to wonder, and to take part in the crazy we call life.
